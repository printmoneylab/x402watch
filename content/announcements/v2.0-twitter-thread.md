## 4/5

v2.0 is a 4-layer split.

L1: seller flags stay on sellers.
L2: (buyer, seller) pair labels.
L3: global guards — buyers with 10+ sellers can't be `self_test` anywhere.
L4: derive global label by tx-weighted majority.

False-positive cost > false-negative cost.
